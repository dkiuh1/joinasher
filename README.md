## Is It Legal?

This is the first thing most wellness business owners want to know, and it's a fair question.

Asher Med is a software and marketing platform, not a medical practice. They don't employ physicians. Licensed medical providers operate independently and carry their own malpractice insurance. The platform is designed to comply with Anti-Kickback Statute (AKS), Stark Law, CPOM regulations, and state telehealth laws. Consultation fees are flat-rate ($30 for 3-month clearance, $20 for 1-month clearance), not tied to referral volume, which is how they structure compliance.

The platform also carries LegitScript approval — a certification used widely in the health and pharmaceutical space to verify legitimate operations.

Most states allow online-only ("asynchronous") approvals. A small number (Arkansas, Mississippi among them) require a short video visit for the first approval — the portal auto-detects which rules apply in each state.
